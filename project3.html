Forgoatten Isles

May 7 2017

The Forgoatten Isles is a first person platformer. Player must traverse through a series of platform puzzles on three islands to find three keys, one on each island, to unlock a grand door and aqcuire the secret pize within. The player can run and jump on different cubes to reach the prize however some cubes behave different and there are hidden traps all over the islands to all new levels of difficulty and challenge to the game.

The Forgoatten Isles was created during the 2017 ToJam from May 5-7. I work in a 5 person team, 3 other programmers and 1 artist. I was the mechanics developer, creating the mechanics for the different blocks, the controls, and hidden traps. I also assisted in level editing and debugging
